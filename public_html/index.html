Portal Route Planner

Instructions

  1. Click on a portal.
  2. Click on the link chain ().
  3. Copy the presented URL into the Portal Planner's "Portal URL's" field.
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 for each portal you wish to visit, pasting each URL on to a new line.
  5. Click Submit.
  6. Copy the Google Maps URL into a browser address bar and submit.
